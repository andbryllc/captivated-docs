---
title: "Adding a Profile Photo"
description: "How to upload, crop, and save a profile photo in your Captivated profile."
---

Adding a profile photo makes it easier to distinguish who is involved in each conversation. To add a profile photo, go to "My Profile", in the "My Profile" tab, scroll down to click on "Select a photo" and select the file you wish to be your profile photo. Once you have selected your photo, you can drag and crop the picture to your desired size. You can also delete your photo and select a new one on the same page if desired. When you are finished, hit the "save" button.
