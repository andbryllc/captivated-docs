---
title: "Why can't I complete a conversation?"
description: "Only the owner of a conversation can complete it; others can leave the conversation or transfer ownership to themselves."
---

If you are not seeing the option to complete a conversation, it's because you are not the conversation owner. Only the owner of a conversation can complete the conversation.

![](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Complete-a-convo-screenshot-1024x695.png)

If you are not the conversation owner, you will simply see the option to Leave the conversation. Leaving the conversation will remove it from your active filter. If necessary, you can use the transfer button to transfer the conversation ownership to yourself the conversation owner in order to complete it.

Learn more about the conversation actions such as [transfer, members, complete, join or leave](https://learn.captivated.works/help/conversations-transferring/) in this step-by-step guide.

If you are in a group [chat](https://learn.captivated.works/team-chat/) within Captivated, you cannot transfer ownership to yourself and complete the conversation. Only the person who started the group chat can complete it. If necessary, you can request that the creator of the conversation remove you from the group chat.
