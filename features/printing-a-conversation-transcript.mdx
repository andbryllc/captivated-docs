## Step by Step Instructions

You can print a conversation transcript or download the transcript as a PDF.

![Print transcript option](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/Screen-Shot-2018-11-30-at-5.22.39-PM-1080x675-1024x640.png)

To print a conversation transcript or download it as a PDF, click on the 3-dot menu below the message field and select "Print Transcript". You can then choose between print and PDF options.
