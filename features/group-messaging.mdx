---
title: "Group Messaging"
description: "Send and receive group messages with up to 10 people in Captivated, initiated from the new message button or the contacts section."
---

Captivated can send and receive group messages up to 10 people. There are a couple of ways to initiate a group message.

1. The green new message button in the top right hand corner of your screen

2. The contacts section in the left-hand menu

Let's review how to send a group message from the green new message button. First navigate to the green new message button in the top right-hand corner of your screen. Ensure you are on the SMS option. The group message option will populate once you have selected more than one contact.

![](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Screenshot-2026-01-08-at-4.57.33%20PM.png)

The second option is initiated via the contacts section on the left-hand side of your screen. You must select the checkbox for more than one contact to initiate a group message.

![](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Screenshot-2026-01-08-at-5.26.58%20PM.png)

After you select "send a group" message a separate screen will pop up where you can verify the numbers and choose the phone number you'd like to send from.

![](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Screenshot-2026-01-08-at-5.07.51%20PM.png)

If someone is opted out of a particular channel/phone number, you cannot send the group message. Remove the contact by hitting the red remove icon or choose a different channel to send from.

![](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Screenshot-2026-01-08-at-5.10.37%20PM.png)

Once all contacts are chosen and opted in, hit continue to send your group message.

![](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Screenshot-2026-01-08-at-5.13.23%20PM.png)

Type your message in the write a message field or choose a template. Click on the info icon at the top right to name your group message (optional) and see the numbers of those you are texting.

![](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/new.png)

If one contact opts out, you will no longer be able to message in the group thread. You will need to initiate a new group thread.

Keep in mind, you cannot text more than 10 people in a single group message.
