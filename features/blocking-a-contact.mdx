## Blocking a Contact Step by Step Instructions

If you are receiving unwanted messages from a contact and/or spammer within Captivated, you can block them and disable incoming messages from routing to Captivated.

To block a contact, open the customer profile by clicking on the information "i" icon in the top right, then choose "block".

![Block a contact](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/Screenshot-2023-09-11-at-3.25.48-PM.png)

## Unblocking a Contact Step by Step Instructions

If you have blocked a contact and you would like to unblock them, simply hit "unblock.

Messages that are sent after unblocking will route to Captivated as expected.

![Unblock a contact](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/Screenshot-2023-09-11-at-3.36.37-PM.png)
