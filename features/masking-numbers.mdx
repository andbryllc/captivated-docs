---
title: "Masking Numbers in your Contact List"
description: "How to enable number masking to hide all but the last four digits of a contact's phone number in Captivated."
---

Want to make your list of contacts in Captivated more secure? Simply reach out to Captivated Support and request number masking be turned on for your business, free of charge.

Number masking will mask all but the last 4 digits of a contact's phone number once it is added to Captivated.

Reasons for turning on number masking:

1. Protect the privacy of the contacts in your list
2. Protect your contact list from an employee who is exiting your company.

Questions? Start chatting with our support team using the Support option in your left-hand menu in Captivated.
