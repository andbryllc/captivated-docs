There are two ways you can create and edit contacts:

1. Using the green new message button
2. Through the contacts section

## Creating Contacts through the Green New Message Button Video Walkthrough

![Creating contacts video walkthrough](https://play.vidyard.com/9KF5rQZudK23T9EGwc8uKk.jpg)

## Creating Contacts through the Green New Message Button Step by Step Instructions

Use the green "New Message" button in the top right corner and start typing the phone number of the contact you wish to add/create. If the number is not in your contacts, you will see an option pop up to add a new contact.

![Add new contact option](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Screenshot-2023-09-25-at-10.04.42-AM.png)

Enter the contact details on the next window and make sure to hit "Save Contact and Continue".

![Enter contact details](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Screen-Shot-2018-12-01-at-5.36.13-PM-1024x806-1.png)

Once you have saved the contact, the message window will pop open for you to text the contact immediately.

## Creating a Contact through the Contacts Tab Video Walkthrough

![Creating a contact through the contacts tab video walkthrough](https://play.vidyard.com/2YVZrvz27VNGkqSgxRSqT9.jpg)

## Creating a Contact through the Contacts Tab Step by Step Instructions

Go to "Contacts" in the left-hand menu and click "+ Add" in the top right corner.

This is a great option for adding a contact that you don't need to send a message to immediately.

In this section, you can also add tags and notes and even adjust a contact's number if needed. E.g. the wrong number was added initially and needs to be changed.

![Add contact through contacts tab](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Screenshot-2023-09-25-at-10.17.04-AM.png)

Input all the information you have available for the contact and make sure to hit save.

Learn more about [tagging contacts here](/features/tagging-contacts).

![Contact information fields](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Screenshot-2023-09-25-at-10.19.13-AM.png)

When you are finished, click the "save" button. You will then have the option to edit the current contact or start a conversation with the newly created contact.

![Edit or start a conversation](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Screenshot-2023-09-25-at-10.24.08-AM.png)

## Editing a Contact Step by Step Instructions

There are also a couple of ways to edit a contact:

1. Using the info icon in an active conversation
2. Through the contacts tab

To edit a contact while currently communicating with them via text, hit the info icon in the top right.

**Note: You cannot edit the contact phone number with this option.**

When you are finished editing, hit save.

![Edit contact via info icon](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Screen-Shot-2018-12-01-at-5.32.19-PM-1-580x1024-1.png)

You can also edit a contact by heading to the contact tab, locating the contact using the search field or filter(s) and clicking on the blue highlighted name.

![Locate contact in contacts tab](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Screenshot-2023-09-25-at-12.28.54-PM.png)

Make sure to hit "edit".

![Hit edit](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Screenshot-2023-09-25-at-12.52.39-PM.png)

![Contact edit fields](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Screenshot-2023-09-25-at-10.19.13-AM.png)

**Why can't I edit the phone number?**

The phone number can only be changed by heading to Contacts tab in the left hand menu.

Yes, learn how [here](/features/importing-contacts). Note: only admins or users that have been given access can bulk import contacts.
