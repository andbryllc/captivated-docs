---
title: "Two-factor Authentication (2FA)"
description: "Enable two-factor authentication to add an extra layer of security to your Captivated account with an SMS code."
---

Two-factor authentication (2FA) provides an extra layer of security for your Captivated account. It's designed to ensure that you are the only person who can access your account, even if someone knows your password.

When 2FA is enabled, you'll receive a text to your cell phone with a 6-digit code that you'll need to input when logging in.

![](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/Screen-Shot-2021-05-10-at-12.14.02-PM.png)

To enable 2FA, go to "My Profile" in the left-hand menu and switch the "Enable Two-Factor Authentication" toggle on.

![](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/Screenshot-2025-08-20-at-3.12.37%20PM-1024x488.png)

- You must have your mobile phone number included under your profile in order to have 2FA enabled.
- Questions? You can chat with Captivated Support via the left-hand menu or text Captivated Support at 317.316.3600
