The Account Contact Card section of Captivated enables you to preview and edit your Account Contact Card. When a new User is set up within your account, whatever information is included in the Account Contact Card is automatically displayed on the User's contact card.

Navigate to the gear icon > Account Contact Card > Edit

![](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Screenshot-2025-08-20-at-10.26.11%20AM.png)

![](https://learn.captivated.works/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Screenshot-2025-08-20-at-10.26.36%20AM.png)

You can choose to Include User Information under the Account Contact Card, which will automatically include the User's name, email and title when they share their contact card in Captivated.

A User's contact card can:

1. Include information from the Account Contact Card (e.g. Dunder Mifflin)
2. Include information from a non-account Team's Contact Card (i.e. Dunder Mifflin Sales)
3. Be completely personalized under the [Contact Card](https://learn.captivated.works/help/sending-your-contact-card/) tab when editing the user in the [Users](https://learn.captivated.works/admin-users) section of Captivated.
